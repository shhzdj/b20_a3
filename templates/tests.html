CSCB20 Tests

Here you will find information and logistics regarding the upcoming tests in CSCB20. Note that as of 11 March 2022, we plan on having all of our term tests and final exam in person.

Midterm

  • There will be one midterm. The midterm will be 75min in length.
  • Date: 13 February, 2022, Monday
  • Time: 5pm - 6:15 pm
  • Location: HW215 and HW216 depending on your last name as shown in the picture

    Preparing for the midterm:


  • Understand all parts of A1.
  • The midterm will cover everything on databases that we did from the start until part way through the week 6 lecture
  • Once you have done some preparation, you can practice on a previous midterm: Term Test
  • You can take a look at some of the past exams papers in the UofT Old Exam repository
